Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-01-09

1. Introduction

BreezeChat Air is a chat-style app designed for private self-expression. The app is designed to work without creating an account.

2. Information We Collect

The developer does not operate an external server to collect your chat content. The app does not collect or transmit your messages to the developer.

  • Chat messages you type
  • Any notes or text you enter in the app
  • In-app totals/counters derived from your usage

The developer cannot view or access your local content.

3. Ads (Free Version Only): Google AdMob

The free version uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may process certain data for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention, such as:

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA) or other device identifiers (depending on your settings)
  • Approximate location (for example, inferred from IP address)
  • Basic ad interaction data (impressions/clicks)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, etc.)

This data is processed by Google or its partners, not by the developer. See Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

When reward is active, ads may be disabled in the UI. (The SDK may still be included in the app binary.)

4. Local Storage

The app is designed so that content can be temporary. If any data is stored, it is stored locally on your device. The developer does not receive it.

5. In-App Purchases

If you choose to purchase a paid version, the transaction is handled by Apple’s App Store (StoreKit). The app does not access or store payment information.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob – ads in the free version.
  • Apple StoreKit – purchase processing (if applicable).

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. AdMob is configured as “Not designed for children.”

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
