About Anniversary Keeper

Anniversary Keeper is a calendar app focused on managing birthdays and anniversaries. You can register yearly and monthly events, set local notifications, and see upcoming important days at a glance.

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Anniversary Keeper is a calendar application designed for managing birthdays and anniversaries. The app works fully offline, requires no account, and stores all user-entered data locally on the device.

2. Information We Collect

The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Photos or videos
  • Contacts
  • Precise location data
  • Search history
  • User-generated text or notes sent to a server

All anniversaries, dates, and notification settings you enter remain on your device only. The developer cannot access this data.

3. Exception: Google AdMob (Free Version Only)

The free version of Anniversary Keeper uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically process certain data for purposes such as ad delivery, frequency capping, or measurement. This can include:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA)
  • Approximate location
  • Basic ad interaction data (for example, impressions or clicks)

These data are processed by Google or its partners, not directly by the developer of this app. For details, please see Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. User Data (Anniversaries, Birthdays, etc.)

All data you enter in the app – such as:

  • Title of the event
  • Date
  • Recurrence settings (yearly or monthly)
  • Notification timing

is stored only on your device. It is never uploaded to any external server by this app.

5. Notifications

Anniversary Keeper uses local notifications only. The app does not use push notifications and does not communicate with external servers to send notifications. All notification data is kept on your device.

6. In-App Purchases

Anniversary Keeper offers a one-time premium upgrade (non-consumable purchase). All purchase processing is handled entirely by Apple’s App Store. The app itself does not access or store any payment information such as credit card numbers.

7. Third-Party Services

The following third-party services are used by the app:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying advertisements in the free version.
  • Apple StoreKit – for processing in-app purchases.

These services may collect technical or advertising-related data under their own privacy policies. Please refer to the respective provider’s documentation for details.

8. Data Sharing

The app does not:

  • Sell personal data to third parties
  • Share user-entered anniversary data outside the device
  • Transfer user data to external servers managed by the developer

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. AdMob is configured as “Not designed for children.”

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
